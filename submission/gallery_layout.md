# Kaggle Project Gallery Layout Guide: MealGenie Live Suite
### Recommended Screenshots & Captions for Submission

To showcase your project effectively, capture these 4 screenshots from your application flow. Below are the recommended screenshot descriptions, titles, and captions to submit in your Kaggle gallery.

---

## Image 1: The Unified Suite Hub
*   **How to Capture:** Capture the root page (`index.html`) displaying the **MealGenie Live Suite** welcome page with its vibrant Electric Indigo and Spring Mint branding.
*   **Recommended Title:** `1. MealGenie Live Suite Hub`
*   **Recommended Caption:**
    > *"The entry point of the MealGenie ecosystem. Users can select the Meal Planner to organize their week at home, or launch the mobile-first AR Shopping Assistant to compare products in real-time at the store."*

---

## Image 2: AI-Powered Weekly Planner
*   **How to Capture:** Generate a plan in the Planner tab, showing the **Dashboard** view with the schedule cards (e.g., Monday Dinner: *Konjac Ramen Bowls*, etc.) and the "Lunch Leftovers" tags highlighted in Spring Mint.
*   **Recommended Title:** `2. Dynamic Weekly Meal Schedule`
*   **Recommended Caption:**
    > *"The weekly planner view showing a custom dinner schedule generated by querying the Google Antigravity backend agent. It matches profile constraints (kid ages, portion scaling) against a private recipe library of 551 items."*

---

## Image 3: Interactive Agent Chat
*   **How to Capture:** Open the floating AI Chat Drawer on the Planner view showing a conversation where you requested a substitution (e.g. *"replace Wednesday's meal with Army Base Stew"*), showing the agent's textual response and the updated calendar cards.
*   **Recommended Title:** `3. Live Chat & Plan Sync`
*   **Recommended Caption:**
    > *"The stateful Agent Chat panel in action. Users can make natural-language requests to modify their weekly plan. The Antigravity Agent executes the query, searches the recipe library using custom tools, and synchronizes the calendar UI on the fly."*

---

## Image 4: AR Shopping Assistant Aisle View
*   **How to Capture:** Open the camera scanner page (`live/index.html`). Pick the "Soda & Beverages" category. Trigger a scan so the gold "TOP PICK" badge appears for Maison Perrier and the red "ALTERNATIVE AVAILABLE" badge shows for Coca-Cola, with the floating AR boxes over the stream.
*   **Recommended Title:** `4. Hands-Free AR Shopping assistant`
*   **Recommended Caption:**
    > *"The mobile-first AR scanner interface. Using client-side TF.js classifiers and Web Speech APIs, shoppers scan items hands-free. A voice command triggers the backend agent to analyze ingredients and highlight the healthiest product choice."*
